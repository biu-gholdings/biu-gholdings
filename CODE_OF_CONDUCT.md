# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

Biu-g Holdings LLC expects professional, respectful participation in this public repository and related community channels.

## Expected behavior

- Use clear, constructive communication  
- Respect differing viewpoints and institutional contexts  
- Focus feedback on work and outcomes, not individuals  
- Protect confidential and personal information  

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation  
- Publishing private or security-sensitive information without authorization  
- Misrepresentation of the company’s regulatory status or investment offerings  

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, reject, or block contributions that violate this code. Serious matters may be referred to appropriate parties.
