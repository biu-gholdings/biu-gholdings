# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you believe you have discovered a security issue related to the **public corporate website** or this repository, please report it responsibly.

**Email:** support@biu-gholdings.org  
**Subject line:** `Security disclosure — biu-gholdings`

Please include:

- A clear description of the issue  
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)  
- Potential impact  
- Your contact information for follow-up  

## What we ask

- Allow reasonable time for review and remediation before public disclosure  
- Do not access, modify, or delete data that does not belong to you  
- Do not perform disruptive testing against production systems  

## Out of scope

This policy covers the **public website and open-source materials** in this repository. It does not cover:

- Third-party services or infrastructure not operated by Biu-g Holdings LLC  
- Social engineering, physical security, or spam/phishing reports unrelated to our code  
- Issues in dependencies already reported upstream (please link the advisory)  

## Response

We aim to acknowledge reports within a reasonable timeframe. Not every report will qualify for a formal advisory; we will communicate our assessment clearly.

We do not operate a public bug-bounty program at this time.

## Safe harbor

Good-faith security research conducted in line with this policy will be treated as authorized. We appreciate contributions that help protect our users and institutions.
