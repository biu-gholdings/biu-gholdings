# Corporate overview

## Parent company

**Biu-g Holdings LLC** (United States) is the global parent and holding platform for a multi-jurisdictional group focused on Angola and Africa.

## Geographic footprint

| Region | Role |
|--------|------|
| **United States** | Global parent, intellectual property, and group coordination |
| **Portugal** | European holding and participation structures |
| **Angola** | National operating alignment and institutional partnerships |

## Portfolio themes

The group’s work is organized around durable themes:

- **Financial and digital infrastructure** — rails, wallets, and institutional market systems  
- **Data and intelligence** — economic visibility and decision support for Angola and Africa  
- **Real-economy platforms** — commerce, mobility, and national-scale services  
- **Education and capacity** — talent development and long-term institutional literacy  
- **Fiduciary stewardship** — structures that support continuity and alignment with national priorities  

## What this document is not

This overview does not describe private ownership percentages, fundraising terms, or investment offerings. Such matters, when applicable, are addressed through appropriate institutional channels.
