# Brand and design

## Visual principles

The public corporate presence uses a restrained, institutional aesthetic:

- Neutral palette (zinc scale) with high contrast for readability  
- Generous whitespace and clear typographic hierarchy  
- Minimal ornamentation — credibility over marketing excess  

## Typography

**Inter** is used for web content via Bunny Fonts. Headings are semibold; body text is comfortable for long-form institutional reading.

## Tone of voice

- Professional and precise  
- Regulator- and institution-aware  
- Long-term and infrastructure-oriented  
- Free of hype, guarantees, or speculative financial claims  

## Bilingual approach

All primary corporate pages are maintained in **English** and **Portuguese** with paired URLs and consistent navigation labels.

## Components

- Shared layout with header navigation and language toggle  
- Card-based sections for mission, pillars, and entity descriptions  
- Forms presented as institutional inquiry interfaces (not investment checkout flows)
