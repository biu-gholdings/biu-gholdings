# Public governance

Biu-g Holdings LLC is structured as a governance-first parent company operating across the United States, Portugal, and Angola.

## Principles

- **Jurisdictional clarity** — roles and responsibilities are defined by entity and geography.  
- **Institutional alignment** — structures are designed for dialogue with regulators, banks, and public institutions.  
- **Accountability** — leadership and governance practices emphasize long-term stewardship.  
- **Measured disclosure** — the public website describes mission and structure at a high level; sensitive operational detail remains protected.  

## Board and leadership

Executive leadership oversees group strategy, technology direction, and institutional relationships. Additional governance practices evolve as the group matures and as independent oversight mechanisms are established.

## Communications

Public statements are intended to be accurate, professional, and appropriate for a global holding company. Material updates are reflected on the corporate website when appropriate.
